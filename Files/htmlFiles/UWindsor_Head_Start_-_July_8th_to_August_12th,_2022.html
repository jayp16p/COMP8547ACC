As per the March 31 announcement, the University of Windsor COVID-19 Vaccination Policy is currently suspended. Based on the recommendation of the Windsor-Essex County Health Unit (WECHU) and in alignment with numerous post-secondary institutions across the province, the University of Windsor’s Mandatory Mask Policy will remain in effect until further notice. Masks remain required in all indoor settings and are strongly recommended outdoors where maintaining a distance of at least 2 metres is difficult. Based on the local public health unit's recommendation, and in the spirit of keeping our campus and community safe, the University of Windsor reminds the UWindsor community and visitors of your individual responsibility to comply with the on-campus mask requirement to reduce the risk of COVID-19 transmission and infection. More information regarding our COVID-19 policy can be found here. Do I need to bring/wear a mask to Head Start? To further support the health and safety of our faculty, staff and students, the University of Windsor requires all employees, students, visitors and contractors to wear a mask or face covering when entering all campus buildings and in common spaces where physical distancing may be difficult (entrances, exits, hallways, stairwells, lounges, washrooms and elevators). You can read the mandatory mask policy here. More information regarding our COVID-19 policy can be found here. Can I bring people with me to Head Start? If you are planning to visit us in person for Head Start, you are permitted to bring up to four additional guests to the Head Start date that you have registered for. Guests will also need to register via our in-person event registration form. If you are planning to visit us virtually for Head Start, guests can register under the virtual head start day registration form. Registrants can bring multiple guests for our virtual head start date. In order for your guest to attend, guests should register under the “guest” option via our online registration platform to secure their spot for our virtual head start date. Guests will receive their own unique login the day of the event in order to access our virtual platform. Virtual/Online FAQS For virtual Head Start, do I need to download or install any information to be able to attend this event? For virtual Head Start, there is no need to download and install any software. You can access the virtual platform via any browser and sign-in to the platform with the email and password that will be sent to you once your registration is completed. For virtual Head Start, is a web cam required to participate in the event? For our programming, it is not mandatory to have your web cams on or utilize one during our event. However, if you would like to video chat with a member of staff or a campus partner, you will need a webcam. You can also use text or voice chat if it’s more convenient. For virtual Head Start, how do I log in the day of the event? Registrants can simply log-in with their email and password that will be sent to them once their registration is completed. For virtual Head Start, who do I contact if I have troubleshooting questions the day of the event? Inside our virtual platform, we will have an information desk and a technical help desk in our virtual lobby in which you can contact in order to answer any inquiries you may have. For virtual Head Start, how will I be able to take information or brochures from the event? Our virtual platform allows anyone who has access to the platform the ability to save different virtual brochures, pamphlets and other documents via the virtual briefcase. Anything saved in the virtual briefcase will be emailed to you directly. For virtual head start, can I invite my friends who attend? Yes! You can share the link to all your friends to sign up. For virtual head start, the times of the live event are not convenient for me. Can I still participate in the event? For your convenience, recordings of all presentations as well as all the resources in the exhibit booths will be available for a month after the event. You can still log in and view the materials and ask your questions by chat or email. For virtual head start, which browser is best for video streaming? For our virtual head start date, any browser should work as long as it’s updated.
