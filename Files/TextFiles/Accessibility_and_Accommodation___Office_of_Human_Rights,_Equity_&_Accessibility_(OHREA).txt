accessibility and accommodation please see the links below regarding information on accessibility laws and the aoda: ontario's accessibility laws and standards, via ontario.ca aoda (accessiblity for ontarioans with disabilities act) of 2005 the university of windsor is committed to providing an accessible learning and working environment for persons with disabilities through the identification and elimination of barriers. this includes respect for dignity, independence, integration and equal opportunity as recognized in the university's policies and the accessibility for ontarians with disabilities act, 2005 (aoda). if you are a student and you would like more information about how the university of windsor can accommodate your needs on campus, please visit student accessibility
